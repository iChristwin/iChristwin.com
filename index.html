Brief Bio

Ifeanyi is a software developer turned entrepreneur. He has been developing technology since he was 18. At the age of 21, he developed proprietary data analytics system with machine learning algorithms capable of analyzing immense amounts of data in real time.

In 2018, He dropped out of collage, joined the open-source community & started his first company -Bazzar

In 2020, He founded Switch Electric and Gas Ltd, an energy company which uses smart metering and machine learning to decentralize access to Electricity in Nigeria.

Switch Electric & Gas Ltd


Switch Electric and Gas Ltd is a renewable energy company providing clean and efficient electricity solutions for households and businesses in Nigeria. We believe we can beat the devastating diesel fuel crisis through technological advancements, as there is way over 300 million Nigerians that could benefit from our clean energy.
Switch envisions an energy future where everyone has access to competitively priced, clean energy.
